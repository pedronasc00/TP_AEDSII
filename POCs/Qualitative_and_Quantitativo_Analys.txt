Qualitative and Quantitative Analysis on the Influence of Diversity on the Productivity of Agile Teams: A Study in the Industry

This article investigates the influence of diversity on the productivity of teams using agile methodologies and also on the quality of the artifacts produced. For this purpose, a qualitative and quantitative research was conducted, in the industry context, involving four teams with different diversity profiles. The study used two data collection methods: a questionnaire organized into three dimensions and metrics applied to data from a development support tool. The results showed that the more diverse teams presented better productivity and quality indicators in their products.
